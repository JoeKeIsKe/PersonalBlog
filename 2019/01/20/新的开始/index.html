Poets to Come

Walt Whitman

Will you seek afar off?

You surely come back at last,

In things best known to you,

finding the best, or as good as the best,

In folks nearest to you finding also the sweetest, strongest, lovingest,

Happiness, knowledge, not in another place, but this place—

not for another hour, but this hour

你要到远处寻觅吗？你最后一定会回来的， 在你所熟悉的东西中找到最好的，或者像最好者一样好， 在你最亲近的人中找到最中意的、最强健的和最爱你的…… 幸福、知识，不在别处而在这里。
